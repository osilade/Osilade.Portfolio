The Shadow Insurance Economy
of Abeokuta South

Survey Design Python Statistical Analysis Data Storytelling Power BI

Why 156 traders trust ajo — but only 11 own formal insurance

A primary research study into informal risk management among market traders in Abeokuta South, Nigeria. Using structured questionnaires across 5 markets, this project maps the trust gap between community savings systems and the formal insurance industry.

The study explores the sociology of financial trust, cultural barriers to insurance adoption, and what insurers could do differently to reach Nigeria’s informal economy.

Currently conducting fieldwork. Survey instruments designed, markets identified, data collection underway. Full interactive dashboard and statistical findings will be published on completion.
